FAQ's

To help farmers water corn without wasting water,
contributing to our mission of water conservation.
When a dry spot in the soil is detected by the sensors,
the corresponding valve that controls the water in that section will activate.
Currently, there is the issue of crops being watered even if they are still wet,
which wastes water and can potentially be bad for the crops.
You set up the control station somewhere you can access it,
replace the valves in your system with our solenoid valves,
and run lines down your drip irrigation pipes, pushing the sensors into the ground in the correct spots.
